Wordle Game

How to Play

  • Guess the hidden 5-letter fruit or vegetable in 6 tries.
  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word from the dictionary.
  • After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was:
A - Right letter, right place A - Right letter, wrong place A - Not in the word
  • Win or lose, you'll discover a benefit of the chosen word!
